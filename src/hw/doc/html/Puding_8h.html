#define DEF_PB   -1024

Default pitch bend variation value.

In the MIDI specification, the pitch wheel may send 16,384 possible variation values. The General MIDI specification recommends that the total range be ± 2 semitones.

The amount of bend to send (in a signed integer format), between -8192 (maximum downwards bend) and 8191 (max upwards bend), center value is 0.

Examples:

1 tone = 8192 1/2 tone = 4096 1/4 tone = 2048 1/8 tone = 1024
